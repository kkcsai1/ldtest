The black triangle represents the consolidated feedback responses of all YTD feedback provided.
    Peer group average
The green triangle represents the average of all the feedback responses for individuals within a peer group (i.e. individuals at the same rank aligned to a service line or sub service line/function within a given area, region or geography). The peer group average serves as a comparative reference to guide conversations about development. It is a static indicator representing a YTD average and appears at the end of each cycle.
    Self-assessment
The blue triangle represents the feedback response provided through the self-assessment.
